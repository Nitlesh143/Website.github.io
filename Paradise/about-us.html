About Us

Welcome to Paradise Aluminium Glass & Construction Works, your premier destination for quality glass and mirror solutions in Labasa, Fiji Islands. Founded by Vinay and managed by Sheenam, we have been serving our community with dedication and excellence.

At Paradise Glass and Mirror, we specialize in providing a wide range of glass and mirror services, including installation, cutting, and custom designs. With years of experience and a commitment to customer satisfaction, we strive to deliver superior craftsmanship and exceptional results.

Our team consists of skilled professionals who are passionate about their work and dedicated to meeting the unique needs of each client. Whether you're looking to enhance the beauty of your home or improve the functionality of your business space, we have the expertise and resources to bring your vision to life.
